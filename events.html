Academic  

OCULUS

  PARTICIPANTS ⇨ 2
  TIME LIMIT ⇨ 2Hrs

➠ Each team will have 2 participants - 1 speaker and 1 interjector.

➠ The topic will be given 5 minutes before the commencement of the event. The topic may or may not be related to IT.

➠ The speaker will have to express his/her views either in favor or against the motion as instructed .

➠ At the time of registration, the participant will be told whether they have to speak in favor or against the motion.

➠ Digital gadgets are strictly prohibited.

➠ Reading from the paper will lead to disqualification.

➠ Time limit for the speaker is 2 to 2.5 minutes.

➠ Once speaker finishes, the rebuttal round will follow wherein the interjector of the other team will question the speaker of the speaking team.

➠ The rebuttal session will be of 1.5 minutes for each team.

➠ The speaker can answer as many questions as possible within the time limit.

➠ The participants will be judged on the basis of content, expression, confidence, pronunciation, poise, and the rebuttal session.
